Brand introduction

Karla international fashion brand, and home to the fashion capital of milan, Italy.Create brand in 1905, rising from 1907 to now, karla fini with rich originality, unique design style favored by the stars and to become the new fashion today.

Brand history

1905 karla fini sprout in milan, Italy, however, under Mr Fini of creating, get the favour of the local celebrities aristocrat, received high praise.In 1907 Mr Fini finally have their own studio, and with "freshman" as the theme, held in the studio in milan runway show, stir the milan fashion at that time.Karla clothing with fashionable element, and gives people a visual extravaganza.The fashion week in 1977, van Karl, the son of Mr Fini inherit Mr Fini's creative spirit, and created a new Carl gianfranco fini, the van after Carl pulled Mr Fini karla Mr Fini was appointed Karl la brand's creative director, and since then Carl la brand into another peak.
